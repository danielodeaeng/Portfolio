About Me

I’m an Industrial Automation & Robotics Systems student who likes to understand how machines really work not just in theory, but by connecting the dots between electrical hardware, PLC ladder logic, and control systems to understand the “why” and “how” behind creating fully functional automation solutions.

I have hands-on experience in a range of Software’s such as Siemens TIA V17, Studio 5000, Cognex Insight Explorer, Wonderware Intouch, VSC and Eplan along with self-taught expertise in SolidWorks, Fusion 360 and AutoCAD Electrical.

I completed my 3rd year Work Placement in BCD Charleville, a pharmaceutical and biopharma process solutions provider with the goal of taking a project from a concept to a fully functional, tested solution. This role involved Schematic verification, Component testing & tagging, VSD Configuration, P&ID walkdowns and FATs while also working closely with engineers across each department which played a key role in delivering large scale solutions.
